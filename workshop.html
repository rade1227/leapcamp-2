Workshop

  • This workshop series is designed to provide job seekers and students with essential skills to excel in both IT and non-IT roles. The series will cover IT skills, communication skills, soft skills, business communication, and aptitude test preparation.

  • Each workshop will offer practical training, interactive sessions, and valuable resources to help participants boost their confidence and competence in their respective fields.

IT Workshops

Basics of IT and Computer Literacy

  • Overview of computer hardware and software
  • Operating systems (Windows, MacOS, Linux)
  • Essential software tools (MS Office, Google Suite)

Introduction to Programming and Coding

  • Fundamentals of programming languages (Python, Java, C++)
  • Basic coding principles and logic
  • Hands-on coding exercises
  • Introduction to version control (Git)

Web Development Fundamentals

  • Basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Introduction to Web Development Frameworks
  • Building a simple website project
  • Database Management

Overview of Databases and SQL

  • Basic database design and querying
  • Practical exercises with SQL

Cybersecurity Essentials

  • Fundamental principles of cybersecurity
  • Common threats and how to mitigate them
  • Best practices for securing data and networks

Advanced IT Skills



  • Introduction to cloud computing (AWS, Azure)
  • Basics of machine learning and AI
  • Overview of DevOps practices




Non - IT Workshops

Verbal Communication Skills

  • Techniques for effective public speaking and presentations
  • Active listening and feedback skills
  • Role-playing and speech exercises

Written Communication Skills

  • Crafting professional emails and reports
  • Business writing principles (clarity, conciseness, tone)
  • Creating impactful resumes and cover letters
  • Interpersonal Skills

Building rapport and networking strategies

  • Conflict resolution and negotiation techniques
  • Teamwork and collaboration in diverse settings

Personal Development Skills

  • Time management and organizational skills
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving strategies
  • Adaptability and resilience in the workplace

Business Etiquette and Professionalism

  • Importance of professional behaviour and ethics
  • Business etiquette in meetings, emails, and phone calls
  • Dressing for success and maintaining a professional image

Advanced Business Communication

  • Crafting persuasive business proposals and presentations
  • Conducting and participating in business meetings
  • Networking strategies and building professional relationships

Aptitude Test Preparation

  • Quantitative aptitude: Basic mathematics and data interpretation
  • Verbal aptitude: Reading comprehension and verbal reasoning
  • Analytical aptitude: Analytical thinking and pattern recognition
  • Test-taking strategies and time management
